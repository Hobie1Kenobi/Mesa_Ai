MESA Investment Analysis

Andreessen Horowitz Web3 Fund | Confidential Investment Memorandum

Executive Summary

After reviewing MESA's blockchain attestation system built on Base, we see substantial potential in this solution for the music rights management space. MESA addresses a crucial pain point in a $50B+ global music industry struggling with rights verification, royalty distribution, and intellectual property protection.

Preliminary Valuation

$30-45M pre-money valuation

Competitive Analysis & Market Opportunity

The music rights management space presents a significant opportunity with persistent inefficiencies that blockchain solutions are uniquely positioned to address:

$50B+

Global music industry with ~$12B in publishing rights/royalties

$2.7B

Estimated annual artist losses from royalty leakage

3-5 Years

Typical timeline to market leadership in Web3 music rights space

Current Solutions

  • BMI, ASCAP, SESAC use centralized databases with high friction
  • Legacy platforms rely on manual verification processes
  • International rights management requires complex cross-border agreements
  • Web3 music rights management is early but growing rapidly

MESA's Competitive Advantages

  • Base blockchain integration provides low-cost, high-speed transactions
  • Ethereum Attestation Service leverages trusted attestation infrastructure
  • Decentralized identity creates trust in a traditionally opaque industry
  • Visual verification adds an intuitive layer to complex blockchain verification

To maximize value creation potential, we recommend these key milestones:

Move from attestation-focused MVP to full rights management platform with intuitive UI/UX for non-technical music creators

Industry Partnerships

Secure 2-3 anchor music label partnerships for validation and initial user acquisition

Monetization Strategy

Develop clear approach to music rights regulations across jurisdictions

Scaling & Enterprise Features

Build enterprise-grade features for major labels and rights organizations

Final Assessment

MESA presents a compelling investment opportunity in the intersection of blockchain and music rights management. The team has demonstrated technical execution ability and a thoughtful approach to a complex industry problem. With proper funding and execution, MESA has the potential to become the standard for blockchain-based music rights management with a plausible path to $100M+ ARR within 5 years.

Key Investment Thesis Points

  • Right team, right time: Strong technical execution in a rapidly evolving space
  • Significant market opportunity: $50B+ industry with clear friction points
  • Technical differentiation: Advanced attestation infrastructure on Base
  • Go-to-market clarity: Clear customer segments and acquisition strategy
  • Capital efficiency: Blockchain infrastructure enables scaling without linear costs
